Simple Stellar Population Datasets
==================================

This pages provides links to several datasets containing tabulations of stellar population spectra as a function of age and metallicity. All files are in the prefered format used by Galacticus, and have been derived from a variety of published sources.

Bruzual & Charlot (2003)
------------------------

Models from `Bruzual & Charlot (2003; MNRAS; 344; 1000) <http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2003MNRAS.344.1000B>`_. Data was downloaded from `http://www.bruzual.org/bc03/Original_version_2003/ <http://www.bruzual.org/bc03/Original_version_2003/>`_.

`Download from Zenodo <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.6338452>`_

* ``SSP_Spectra_BC2003_lowResolution_imfChabrier.hdf5``: Low-resolution, `Chabrier <http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2001ApJ...554.1274C>`_ IMF;
* ``SSP_Spectra_BC2003_lowResolution_imfSalpeter.hdf5``: Low-resolution, `Salpeter <http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1955ApJ...121..161S>`_ IMF;
* ``SSP_Spectra_BC2003_highResolution_imfChabrier.hdf5``: High-resolution, `Chabrier <http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2001ApJ...554.1274C>`_ IMF;
* ``SSP_Spectra_BC2003_highResolution_imfSalpeter.hdf5``: High-resolution, `Salpeter <http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1955ApJ...121..161S>`_ IMF;
